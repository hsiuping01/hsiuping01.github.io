How does it work?


1. Meet the requirements
  • • Required to live within the 1st Supervisorial District and not within the City of Los Angeles.
  • • Meet the income qualification.
  • • Proof of residence can be an electric or gas bill.
  • • Unhoused folks are exempt from presenting proof of residence.
  • • Proof of income can be paystubs of three months.
  • • Seniors may provide Social Security Award or Supplemental Security Income (SSI).
  • • ID is preferred but not required.


2. Register
  • • Contact Brissa Sanchez at (323) 726-7998 to register.
  • • Registration is in person at the Eastmont Community Center.
  • • You will fill out an intake pack list.
  • • After registering, you will get an appointment to pick up the food.


3. Pick up your food
  • • Visit us at 701 Hoefner Avenue, East Los Angeles, CA 90022.
  • • Our pick-up times are Fridays from 8 a.m. to 10 a.m.
  • • It's advisable to bring a cart to help carry the food you'll receive.
  • • Staff will check appointments and provide numbered tickets to confirm space in line.
